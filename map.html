Cape Town, South Africa

The Mother City

Known as The Mother City, Cape Town is South Africa's legislative capital, i.e. it is home to the country's legislature. (The executive offices and judicial offices are located in other cities throughout the country.)

Originally established as a trading outpost for the Dutch East India company, Cape Town is now one of the most culturally vibrant cities in South Africa and, indeed, the entire continent.

Here's a few things the city is known for:

  • Incredible natural beauty, including Table Mountain National Park
  • A world renowned wine scene
  • Food with an unrivaled international flair
  • The Bo-Kapp Neighborhood and its colorful houses

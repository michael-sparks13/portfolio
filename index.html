    About me

    I'm a software engineer and cilmate journalist in New York. My journalism focuses on the intersection of climate change and public lands. In fact, I started an entire site dedicated to just that: CrowdedParks.org. I've also been published in the number one site for national parks news, NationalParksTraveler.org

    As a self-taught developer, I think about engineering as another tool to produce great journalism. For instance, I noticed there were very few, if any, sites that deeply focused on how important public lands are to the fight against climate change, so I built one myself.

    I can work in:

    • Python, including Pandas, Beautiful Soup, Plotly
    • JavaScript, HTML/CSS (obviously)
    • React

About Me

I am passionate about climate, the environment, and the economic transition needed to mitigate the worst of the climate crisis. I am also deeply interested in extreme weather: no matter what we do in the future, we have done so much damage to the environment already that we must learn how to live with the increasingly common occurrence of severe weather and storms.

I created the website CrowdedParks.org to report on national parks and climate, aiming to raise awareness about environmental issues. I also covered COP28 on my substack, Sparks On Climate. I am a current board member at the North American Cartographic Information Society (NACIS). I live in the NYC area with my family and am a five-time IRONMAN finisher (2.4mi swim, 112 mi bike, 26.2 mi run).
